Trade In Your Boat with Confidence

Trading in your boat doesn’t have to be complicated. At Starboard Choice Marine, we make the process simple, transparent, and fair—so you can move into your next boat with confidence. Whether you’re upgrading, downsizing, or ready for something new, our experienced team will provide an honest evaluation and competitive trade value based on current market conditions.

Our Simple 3-Step Process

Tell Us About Your Boat

Share a few details about your boat, including the make, model, year, and condition. Photos and service history help us provide the most accurate estimate.

Get a Fair Market Evaluation

Our team reviews your information and determines a competitive trade-in value using market data and condition—no pressure, no surprises.

Trade In & Upgrade

Apply your trade-in value toward your next boat and let us handle the paperwork. We make the transition smooth so you can focus on getting back on the water.
