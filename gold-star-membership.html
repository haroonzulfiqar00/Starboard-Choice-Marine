Become A Gold Star Member

What Is the Gold Star Membership?

The Gold Star Membership is an exclusive benefit for customers who purchase a new boat and motor combination from Starboard Choice Marine. When you buy new from us, you automatically become a Gold Star Member—and you remain a member for as long as you own that boat. It’s our way of rewarding your trust with priority service, dependable scheduling, and added peace of mind throughout every boating season.

Automatic Membership with New Boat Purchase

There’s no sign-up, no annual fee, and no hassle. When you purchase a new boat/motor package from Starboard Choice Marine, you are immediately enrolled in the Gold Star Membership program. It’s built-in value designed to support you long after you leave the showroom.

Exclusive Service for Our Boat Owners

By focusing Gold Star benefits on customers who purchase new boats from us, we’re able to deliver faster turnaround times, consistent service, and reliable scheduling—especially during peak boating months. This allows us to maintain the high standards our customers expect and deserve.

Benefits of membership

Priority Hoist Installation & Removal

Gold Star Members receive priority scheduling for boat hoist installation after spring ice-out and prompt removal at the end of the season. Your time on the water matters, and this priority access helps ensure your boat is ready when the season starts—and taken care of when it ends.

Professional Barge & Recovery Services

Gold Star Members also receive access to our state-of-the-art work barge for equipment recovery. Whether it’s due to severe weather, collisions, or unexpected situations, our team can safely and efficiently recover submerged equipment without causing damage.

Experience the Gold Star Difference

When you purchase your next new boat from Starboard Choice Marine, you’re not just buying a boat—you’re gaining long-term service advantages through our Gold Star Membership. It’s one more way we stand behind our products and our customers.
