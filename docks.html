Hoist & Barge Services

Looking for reliable hoist and barge services in Southern Michigan? Starboard Choice Marine, located in Edwardsburg, MI, proudly serves boaters from Niles, Dowagiac, and beyond. We provide timely boat hoist installation after spring ice-out and removal at the end of the season, prioritizing customers who store with us or purchase a new Great Lakes Lift to ensure fast, dependable service.

Our state-of-the-art work barge allows us to safely and efficiently recover submerged equipment caused by severe weather, collisions, or unexpected situations—without causing damage. When you need professional hoist or barge services, you can count on Starboard Choice Marine.

SERVICES

Installation

The team at Starboard Choice Marine can install a wide variety of lifts, including boat and personal watercraft lifts. We only offer this service to customers who store their boats at our facility, so you can count on fast and reliable service.

Delivery & Transportation

Don’t have a lift yet? Our team can assist you in choosing the right lift for your needs. We can also deliver the equipment of your choice to almost any location – our work barge even allows us to reach areas not accessible by land!

Removal Services

Lift removal is quick and easy with the help of our work barge. We can expertly remove lifts from any location, without damaging your equipment in the process.

Repair & Maintenance Services

Does your lift need servicing? The team at Starboard Choice Marine can handle routine maintenance and repairs for almost any lift. No matter what kind of lift you own, it will be in good hands with our capable technicians!
