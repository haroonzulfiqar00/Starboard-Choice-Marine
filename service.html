SERVICES PROVIDED

BOAT DETAILING

Detailing, polishing, and waxing are not to be overlooked. These are important maintenance tasks that will improve the overall condition of your boat. During the detailing process, every component of your boat will be professionally cleaned and inspected. Regular detailing will keep your boat clean and pristine, and preserve its resale value!

ENGINE REPAIR

Our team of six certified technicians and highly efficient service department are here to keep your engine running at its best. From diagnostics to full repairs, Starboard Choice Marine is the right choice for dependable, professional service.

BOAT WINTERIZATION

Boat winterization is an important part of boat maintenance here in Michigan! This is the process of making sure your boat is protected from the elements while it waits out the winter in storage. Our boat winterization service includes removing any water from the vessel, fogging the engine, replacing the gear oil, and adding a fuel stabilizer to the gas tank.

ENGINE INSTALLATION

Installing a new engine is a great way to breathe new life into your old boat! Of course, engine installation is easier said than done, so this task is best left to the professionals. We can handle every aspect of engine installation here at Starboard Choice Marine.

Boat Storage

Looking for a safe, secure place to store your boat this upcoming winter? Look no further than Starboard Choice Marine! We have a convenient outdoor storage facility at our location on Lake Pleasant. Winterize and store your boat with us, and you can count on it being ready for action when boating season rolls back around!
